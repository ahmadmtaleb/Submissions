Welcome to this interactive story, where you will find yourself

"Cheese" is a metaphor for what we want to have in life, whether it is a job, a relationship, money, a big house, freedom, health, recognition, spiritual peace, or even an activity like jogging or basketball.
Each of us has his own idea of what Cheese is, and we pursue it because we believe it makes us happy. If we get it, we often become attached to it. and if we lose it, or it's taken away it can be traumatic.

"World" is your world is a maze where you ave stations A, B, C, D, ...Z and you are looking for cheese to nourish yourself and make yourself happy. "House" is your starting point from where you explore this maze. "Running gear" the running gear is your only tool in this world. "Jaafar" is your avatar in this story. So, take care of him!

Every morning Jaafar dressed in his running gear and headed over to Cheese station C where he founds his own kind of cheese. It was a large store of cheese that eventually Jaafar moved his house to be closer to it and build a social life around it.
"Having cheese makes you happy!"
One morning Jaafar arrived at Cheese Station C and discovered that there was no cheese.
